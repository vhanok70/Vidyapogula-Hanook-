About Hanook Global Traders
Hanook Global Traders is a Kurnool-based merchant exporter specializing in quality metals — we connect verified Indian mills and recyclers to international buyers with transparent pricing, strict documentation and reliable logistics.

Quality & Documentation

Mill Test Certificates (MTC), Certificate of Origin, Packing List, Commercial Invoice, inspection reports (SGS/Intertek) on request.

Logistics & Terms

CIF / FOB / EXW terms, sea freight container and bulk packing, fast handling and customs clearance support.

Flexible Supply
